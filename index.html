Surrender to the Charms of Kerala's Backwaters

ABOUT

Embark on an unforgettable journey through Kerala's picturesque backwaters on our comfortable and well-equipped houseboats. Immerse yourself in the serene beauty of lush green landscapes, tranquil waters, and charming villages. Let the gentle sway of the boat and the soothing sounds of nature transport you to a world of relaxation and rejuvenation. Our expert crew ensures your comfort and safety while you savor delicious local cuisine and experience the warm hospitality of Kerala. Whether you seek a romantic getaway, a family adventure, or a peaceful retreat, our houseboats offer an enchanting escape into the heart of Kerala's backwater bliss.

Best Sea Food

Indulge in the rich flavors of Kerala's backwater cuisine as you embark on an unforgettable journey through serene waters. Our sumptuous meals are a celebration of local traditions and flavors, meticulously crafted to tantalize your taste buds. From fragrant seafood curries to aromatic rice dishes, each bite is a culinary voyage through the heart of Kerala's culinary heritage. Join us aboard our houseboats and savor the essence of Kerala's backwater cuisine, where every meal is a feast for the senses. Book your culinary adventure today and immerse yourself in the flavors of the backwaters!

Experience Kerala Like a Local

Discover more than just houseboats with our homestay facilities, offering an authentic Kerala experience. Nestled amidst the breathtaking backwaters, our cozy homestays provide a haven where you can truly live like a local. Welcomed by our gracious hosts, you'll find yourself immersed in genuine Kerala hospitality from the moment you arrive. Indulge in traditional yet comfortable accommodations, complemented by homemade Kerala cuisine bursting with local flavors. Whether you're seeking cultural immersion or simply a tranquil retreat, our homestays promise an enriching experience that goes beyond the ordinary. Embrace the heart and soul of Kerala with our exclusive homestay offerings.
